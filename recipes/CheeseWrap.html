Lasagna

Description

Easy cottage cheese wrap

Keto friendly and high protein!


Ingredients:

  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup high protein cottage cheese
  • 1/4 tsp Everything Bagel seasoning
  1. Preheat the oven to 380F. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment and spray with nonstick spray
  2. Add eggs and cottage cheese to the jar of a blender; blend on high until smooth, about 30 seconds. Pour mixture onto the prepared pan in a roughly rectangular shape. Sprinkle bagel seasoning over mixture.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove immediately. Allow to cool about 5 minutes before removing wrap from parchment.
